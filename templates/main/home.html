ABOUT SAVIJO GEO-ENGINEERING CO.LTD

INTRODUCTION
COMPANY BACKGROUND

Savijo Geo-Engineering Company Limited is a Tanzania registered private company founded in 2021. Currently, Office is located at Kibugumo - Kigamboni, near Kibugumo Dispensary, Dar es salaam, Tanzania. The aim of the company is providing consulting services to the fields of geotechnics, mining, geology, survey, environment, water resources, waste management, construction and Oil & Gas.

Our Mission

To provide professionally high quality and efficient technical services to the mining, energy, hydro and engineering construction industries, fostering a safe, healthy and environmentally friendly working environment that contributes effectively to the sustainable development of the entire mining, energy and construction industries.

Our Vision

To be recognised as the best company in the provision of specialised expert advice and solutions in the fields of geotechnics, mining, geology, survey, environment, water resources, waste management and Oil & Gas in Tanzania and internationally..

OUR GOALS

  • To provide solution to geotechnical problems assocciated with mining, construction and other projects that require geotechnical expert advice at any level.
  • To provide consulting services to the mineral, energy and engineering construction sectors of the economy. Specifically focus on initial mineral and fossil fuels prospecting and exploration, mines and project development, mining operation and closure phases, and rehabilitation of disturbed areas. For the construction industry, services assocciated with site investigation, geotechnical, hydrological and hydro-geological investigations.
  • To serve the small, medium and large scale mine sectors of the industry, through the conduct of technological safe, efficient, and environmentally sound practices.
  • To provide training in aspects of safety, health and environment in mine for safe mining and project continuity.
  • To provide training in the field of Geotechnics, Mining, Geology, Survey, Environment, Water resources, Construction and Oil and Gas. Consultation in company and dispatch management.
